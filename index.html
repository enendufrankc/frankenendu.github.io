About Me

As a driven and dedicated Machine Learning Engineer and Data Scientist, I thrive on solving complex real-world problems with data. My passion lies in taking raw, unstructured data and transforming it into meaningful insights that drive business decisions.
My journey with data began in university, where I discovered my love for it while utilizing it to win a student union election. Since then, I have dedicated my career to mastering the art of data manipulation and analysis. My practical hands-on experience has given me a deep understanding of data technologies, ranging from the basics of Excel to the cutting-edge world of Machine Learning.
With a Master's degree in Data Science and Artificial Intelligence, I have a theoretical foundation that complements my practical expertise. I have honed my skills working with a diverse range of businesses across various industries, including travel and tourism, technology support, retail, edutech, and fintech.
I am constantly seeking new challenges that push me to my limits and allow me to grow as a professional. As such, I am eager to work with organizations that present me with fresh and exciting data challenges every day. My goal is to use my skills to help businesses make data-driven decisions that have a real impact on their bottom line. Let me show you how I can unlock the full potential of your data!
